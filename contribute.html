Collaborate with us

Are you making a difference in the world? We’d love to meet with you to learn about what you do, and how we can help support you. We primarily provide short film production and distribution services get in touch to see how we could help you.

Support Us

There are multiple ways you can support us, from watching and sharing our films to becoming a partner organisation.

Partner with us

Support your favorite charities by becoming a mentor, partner or sponsoring a film. We are on the look out for the right mentors, and long term partnerships to help us run a more effective organisation. Sponsorship will help us fund the shoot, software, and equipment necessary for filmmaking. We love to celebrate our sponsors, get in touch to find out how we can work together.

Donations welcome

Film for Change has a Give a little account so you can donate right away, even anonymously.

Distribution

Film for Change currently distributes our short films through our collaborators own media channels, on permanent screens at public venues, at public screening events, on this website, and social media. We’re always looking for new ways to get our films out to multiple audiences, get in touch to collaborate with us on new creative distribution strategies.

Volunteer

At Film for Change we believe everyone can use their skills to help change the world. From filmmaking to accounting, we are a community of passionate people who support Wellington’s changemakers. If you want to support our mission contact us or come to one of our meetup events and we’ll work together to create positive social change.
